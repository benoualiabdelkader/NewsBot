Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Creating an account is easy! Click the profile icon in the top right corner, then select "Sign Up". Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address. You can also sign up using your Google or social media accounts.
How do I navigate through different news sections?
Use the navigation menu at the top of the page to access different sections: Home (top stories), Categories (news by topic), Live (live streams), and Search. You can also use the search icon to find specific topics.
Is NewsBot free to use?
Yes! NewsBot is completely free to use. All features including news reading, AI chatbot, search, and personalization are available at no cost.

Account & Settings

How do I change my notification preferences?
Go to your Account page by clicking your profile picture, then select the "Preferences" tab. Here you can customize which categories you want notifications for and how frequently you receive them.
How do I save articles to favorites?
When reading an article, look for the heart icon or "Add to Favorites" button. Click it to save the article. You can view all your saved articles in the Favorites section of your account.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account at any time. Go to Account Settings > Security tab, and you'll find the option to delete your account. This action cannot be undone and will remove all your data.

Features

How does the search feature work?
Our search feature combines news articles, images, and videos related to your query. Simply type your search term in the search box and press Enter. You can search for topics, people, events, or specific news sources.
What are the different news categories available?
NewsBot covers multiple categories including World News, Technology, Sports, Business, Health, Entertainment, Science, and Politics. You can browse these categories from the main navigation or customize your preferences.
How do live news streams work?
The Live section shows current news broadcasts from major networks. Click "Watch Now" on any stream to open it in a video player. These are real-time broadcasts covering breaking news and current events.

AI Assistant

How do I use the AI chatbot?
Click the chat icon in the bottom right corner to open the AI assistant. You can ask questions about current events, request article summaries, or get explanations about complex topics. Just type your question and press Enter.
What kind of questions can I ask the AI?
You can ask about current events, request news summaries, ask for explanations of complex topics, get historical context, or ask for analysis of trends. The AI is designed to help you understand and discuss news topics.
Is the AI information always accurate?
While our AI is trained on reliable sources, always verify important information from authoritative news sources. The AI is designed to be helpful but may occasionally have limitations or provide incomplete information.

Troubleshooting

The page is loading slowly. What should I do?
Try refreshing the page or checking your internet connection. If the problem persists, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. You can also try disabling browser extensions temporarily.
Images are not loading properly. How can I fix this?
This is usually due to slow internet connection or ad blockers. Try disabling ad blockers, refreshing the page, or checking your internet connection. Most images have fallback placeholders if the original fails to load.
The AI chatbot is not responding. What's wrong?
This could be due to server issues or high traffic. Try refreshing the page and attempting your query again. If the problem persists, the AI service might be temporarily unavailable for maintenance.
