Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the news information provided by NewsBot?
NewsBot sources information from verified, reputable news organizations and uses AI to fact-check and cross-reference stories. Our AI is trained to identify and filter out misinformation while prioritizing trusted sources.
Is NewsBot free to use?
Yes! NewsBot is completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to reliable news information. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core news service will always remain free.
How does the AI chatbot work?
Our AI chatbot uses Google's Gemini technology to understand your questions about current events. It can summarize articles, explain complex topics, provide context, and answer questions about news stories from around the world.
Can I customize my news preferences?
Absolutely! You can customize your news preferences in your account settings. Choose your favorite categories, set notification preferences, and save articles for later reading.
How often is the news updated?
NewsBot updates continuously throughout the day. Breaking news appears within minutes, while other stories are refreshed every hour to ensure you always have the latest information.
